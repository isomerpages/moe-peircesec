<div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Header.jpg"></div><h4>Announcement: Peirce Secondary School Secondary 1 posting and registration matters 20-21 December 2023</h4><p>Dear <strong>Secondary One Ladies and Gentlemen,</strong></p><p><strong>Congratulations</strong> on completing PSLE and your primary school journey!</p><p>And a very warm <strong>Welcome to our Peirce Family!</strong></p><p>Please note that students do not need to report to their posted secondary schools upon receiving their S1 posting results. However, there will be school personnels (Secondary 1 Form Teachers, Year Heads, School Counsellor, and Special Needs Education Officer) in school on Thu 21 Dec, from 9am to 12pm, if you wish to speak to any of them. Please note that only on Thu 21 Dec, both the front and back gates will be open. On other days, only the front gate will be open.</p><p>The school will be using&nbsp;<strong>Parents Gateway (PG)</strong>&nbsp;to communicate with you, useful information on how to prepare your child/ward for the first day of school.</p><p>For your child’s/ward’s&nbsp;<strong>2024 Sec 1 Class allocation</strong>, you will be able to check via&nbsp;<strong>Parents Gateway</strong>&nbsp;from 21 December 2023.</p><p>Please note the points below and take the appropriate action. <strong>Thank you</strong> for your understanding.</p><p>For further clarification please <a href="https://www.peircesec.moe.edu.sg/contact-us/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Contact us</a>.</p><p><strong>1.</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Mass Appeal Exercise</strong></p><p>For Mass Appeal Exercise, transfer appeals will&nbsp;<strong>only</strong>&nbsp;be considered on exceptional basis for students with:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Serious medical conditions, such as chronic heart conditions or kidney problems.</p></li></ul><p>All appeals must be accompanied by relevant supporting documents, such as medical reports, letters from doctors etc. They should be submitted to Peirce Secondary School General Office <strong>before 12 noon on 21 Dec</strong>. The staff will help to file your appeal. The appeal results will be released in early January. In the interim, students should still attend school from 2 Jan 2024. Please call the school at 64576454 for clarifications.</p><p><strong>2.</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Registration</strong></p><p><strong>All parents are to register their child via Parent Gateway (PG)</strong>, even if they wish to transfer their child out for non-medical reasons. Students seeking transfers should continue to attend their posted secondary school until the transfer is approved. Transfers are subjected to vacancies at the requested transfer school.</p><p>Registration can be done <strong>online</strong> or <strong>on site at school</strong>.</p><p><strong>(a)</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Online registration (from 20 Dec, 12pm onwards)</strong></p><p>For online registration, parents/ guardians must respond to the PG welcome notification by clicking “online registration” and filling up the student profile form on Form@SG by 21 Dec 12 noon.</p><p>If you do not have PG, please go to the following <a href="https://go.gov.sg/pss-s1registrationlink23" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">link</a> for the online registration.</p><p>For parents who are unable to register online, please call the school at 64576454. Our staff will either assist you to register online or note your registration.</p><p><strong>(b)</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Onsite registration</strong></p><p>You and your child/ ward can come to Peirce Secondary School on 21 Dec for registration as well as to purchase books and uniforms.</p><p><strong>3.</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Secondary One student from other schools appealing to Peirce Secondary School (Non-Medical reason)</strong></p><p>Primary 6 students who have just received their secondary school posting in 2023 may use this form in the following link: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/appealfortransfertopssdec23" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/appealfortransfertopssdec23</a> to appeal for transfer to Peirce Secondary School. Their PSLE score should meet the school's cut-off point of the posting year.</p><p>Transfer will be subject to available vacancies and the school’s admission criteria.</p><p><strong>Notes:</strong></p><p>(1)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The personal information provided in this form will be solely used for the purpose of appealing for secondary school transfer based on non-medical reasons.
