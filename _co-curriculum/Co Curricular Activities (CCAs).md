<p>The CCA programme at Peirce provides students a platform to learn new
skills, develop their talents and make new friends. Besides, students also
learn core values and enhance their social and emotional competencies and
the emerging 21st&nbsp;Century Competencies.</p>
<p>The school has four broad categories of CCAs, namely Sports, Visual &amp;
Performing Arts, Uniformed Groups and Clubs.&nbsp; Each CCA has its specific
objectives. The Sports CCAs aim to develop robustness, fair play and team
spirit in the athletes. The Visual and Performing Arts CCAs instill in
students a sense of graciousness and an appreciation for the arts. The
Uniformed Groups (UG) aim to inculcate in cadets self-reliance, resilience,
discipline and a spirit of service to others. The clubs provide opportunities
for&nbsp;students to explore and develop their interests.</p>
