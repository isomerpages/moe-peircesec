<p><strong>Goals of Education and Career Guidance (ECG)</strong>
</p>
<p>In Peirce, we aim to equip our students with the necessary knowledge,
skills and values so that they are&nbsp;<strong>Future Ready</strong>&nbsp;–
have a sense of purpose in life, develop the dispositions of adaptability
and lifelong learning so as to be able to navigate education and career
pathways purposefully and take on the challenges of the future, including
the world of work.</p>
<p><strong>Guiding Principles in planning of ECG Experiences:</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Student Centricity&nbsp;– learning experiences are planned based on students’
needs, interests and aspirations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Intentionality&nbsp;– learning experiences are planned with specific learning
outcomes in mind.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coherence&nbsp;– learning experiences enable students to connect, transfer,
and apply learning across different key student development experiences.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaboration&nbsp;– establish meaningful partnership with parents, PSEIs
and other external organisations</p>
</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>ECG Counselling</strong>
</p>
<p>Our Education and Career Guidance (ECG) Counsellor, Mr Colin Tan, is in
the school&nbsp;every Tuesday and Thursday&nbsp;to support students in
making informed decisions on their education and career pathways.</p>
<p>Students (and their parents) who would like to find out more about their
post-secondary education options, subject choices, career interests, or
discover their strengths and interests, can have a chat with our ECG Counsellor.</p>
<p>To make an appointment, please click here:&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/ecg-pss" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/ecg-pss</a>&nbsp;or
scan the QR Code below. You may also email Mr Colin Tan at&nbsp;<a href="mailto:colin_tan_thian_hock@schools.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">colin_tan_thian_hock@schools.gov.sg</a>
