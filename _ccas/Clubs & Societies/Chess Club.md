<p>The Chess Club was started in 2025 as an interest group. In 2026, it is
formally launched as a CCA. Students may opt to join the Chess Club as
their main CCA, subject to availability of places. Other interested students
may also request to join the Chess Club as a 2nd CCA, or participate in
some activities out of interest.
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Learning outcomes:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Chess develops foresight, planning, and decision-making skills as players
anticipate opponentsâ€™ moves and evaluate multiple outcomes enhancing overall
problem-solving abilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Regular practice teaches perseverance through losses, patience during
long matches, and focus under pressure building essential character traits.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CCA involvement fosters collaboration in school teams and tournaments,
boosting confidence, sportsmanship, and social bonds.</p>
</li>
</ul>
